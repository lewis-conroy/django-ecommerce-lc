Crafty Devil's Story

Crafty Devil is an independent crafting materials supplier based in Glasgow. We were founded in 1806, when Harold Wagner von Devil III emmigrated from Transylvania to Glasgow to offer his expertise to the bustling carpentry industry in Scotland at the time. However, Devil was surprised to find that it wasn't only carpenters who were in need of his wares, and so he expanded his business to provide materials for artists, seamstresses, and many more expert crafters throughout the nation.



Crafty Devil eventually earned its fame when it was commissioned by NASA to fashion the space-suits worn by astronauts in 1969 on the Apollo 11 mission to visit the moon. The suits were created out of the most pristine materials in the country, and as you well know, worked flawlessly during the historic lunar mission. This earned Crafty Devil and Harold von Devil himself world-recognised accolade, and eventually knighthood in over 10 countries across the continent. Harold is still alive to this day, and not only is he still creating brilliant pieces of work, but also supplying you with his best materials.


Simply put, we put the materials in the hands of those who can make something of it. Just as a restaurant gives its chefs quality ingredients to make delicious meals with, we provide expert carpenters with quality wood, dedicated artists quality paint, and master sculpters quality clay for them to hammer, stroke and carve into pieces of art. With unbeatable prices, we have our customers at the forefront of our mind at all times, and so we strive to give you the highest level of service possible to get your work looking as amazing as it can.


We sell paint, fabrics, wood, adhesives, you name it. If you need it for a crafts project, the chances are we have it in stock. To browse our wares, hit the products button, or why not pop in store?
